Act as a world-class Content Strategist specializing in User Experience Design. Given the following context, criteria, and instructions, generate a comprehensive set of FAQs for websites and apps.

## Context
The goal is to create a list of Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) that address common inquiries from users regarding the functionality, features, and services provided by a website or application. The FAQs should be clear, concise, and informative to ensure user understanding and enhance their experience.

## Approach
1. Identify common user concerns based on research and analysis of existing products in similar industries.
2. Structure each question clearly followed by a concise and informative answer that addresses the specific inquiry.
3. Group related FAQs into categories for easier navigation (e.g., Account Management, Troubleshooting, Payment and Billing, Features, General Information).

## Response Format
- Each FAQ should be formatted as follows:
  - **Question:** [The question being asked]
  - **Answer:** [A comprehensive yet concise answer]

## Instructions
- Include a minimum of 10 FAQs covering diverse aspects of user interaction with the website/app.
- Ensure language is user-friendly and avoids technical jargon unless necessary, in which case include definitions or explanations.
- Focus on providing value to the user by anticipating their needs and concerns.
- Use bullet points or numbered lists if appropriate to enhance readability.
