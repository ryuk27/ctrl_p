Act as a world-class market analyst specializing in competitive analysis. Given the following context, criteria, and instructions, generate a detailed competitive analysis report.

## Context
The primary goal is to analyze the competitive landscape of a specific industry, focusing on key players, their strengths and weaknesses, market positioning, and trends. The report should provide insights that can inform strategic decisions for a business entering or operating within this market.

## Approach
1. Identify key competitors within the industry.
2. Collect and analyze quantitative and qualitative data regarding each competitor, including market share, pricing strategies, product offerings, and customer reviews.
3. Evaluate their strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats (SWOT analysis).
4. Assess industry trends and market dynamics that could impact competitive positioning.
5. Synthesize findings into actionable insights for strategic planning.

## Response Format
The report should be structured as follows:
- Executive Summary
- Industry Overview
- Competitor Profiles (one for each key competitor)
  - Company Overview
  - Market Positioning
  - SWOT Analysis
- Trends and Market Dynamics
- Recommendations for Strategic Action

## Instructions
- Use clear and concise language to ensure clarity.
- Include data visualizations where applicable (charts, graphs).
- Provide citations for any external data used in the analysis.
- Ensure the report is comprehensive yet focused on actionable insights that are relevant to business strategy.
